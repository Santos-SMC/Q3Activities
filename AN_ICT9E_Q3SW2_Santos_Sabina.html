Title: Diary ng Panget: The Movie

Director: Andoy Ranay

Date Released: April 2, 2014

Why This Movie? "Diary ng Panget" was one of the first Filipino movies I watched as a kid, and I still listen to its OST playlist now.		 An underprivileged young woman works as a personal maid for a wealthy and appealing young guy. At first, the two do not get along, but as their lives become intertwined, so does their relationship. "It had its charm...but made my head hurt."

Title: Ang Babaeng Allergic sa Wifi

Director: Jun Lana

Date Released: August 15, 2018

Why This Movie? "Ang Babaeng Allergic sa Wifi" was a movie I never wanted to watch due to its cringe title, but when I did, I could not help but bawl my eyes out.		 Norma, a social media-obsessed youngster, is transferred to an area without service where she must manage her boyfriend's long-distance relationship because she has electromagnetic hypersensitivity, which prevents her from being around technology. "One million followers in the virtual world is nothing compared to that One special someone in the real world."
- richarddillomes		 Stars: 6.1/10
Title: Seven Sundays

Director: Cathy Garcia-Molina

Date Released: October 11, 2017

Why This Movie? "Seven Sundays" is a movie that made me realize how little time I have to spend with my loved ones and how I should resolve my problems with them before we break apart.		 The narrative opens with the father of four children telling his children that he will pass away shortly. This father is a widower who misses his adult children's companionship and feels lonely. After learning of their father's illness, the siblings resolve to spend time together. Despite their differences and personal concerns, they try to do this for their father. "WAS A GREAT MOVIE , ENDING SCREW IT UP"
- iloverain-32527		 Stars: 7.6/10
